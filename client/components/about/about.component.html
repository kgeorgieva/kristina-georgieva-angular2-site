About

I was supervised by Prof. Andries Engelbrecht and did my research in temporal data clustering using Differential Evolution and Particle Swarm Optimisation.

Though I am originally from Sofia, Bulgaria; I grew up in Caracas, Venezuela. For this reason, I speak 3 languages, namely Spanish, English and Bulgarian.
